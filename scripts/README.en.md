* Objective : 

Develop a technical analysis system for 3 major US stocks: AAPL, JPM, MSFT (Apple Inc, JPMorgan Chase & Co , Microsoft Corporation) covering 500+ days of historical data (2022-2024), with calculation of 7 technical indicators (MA20/MA50, RSI,  Full Bollinger Bands (BB_Middle, BB_Upper , BB_Lower), Volume) and generation of 4 professional visualizations. 

* Results Analysis of Technical Indicators :
      * RSI Analysis : 
       * AAPL (Apple) :
             Looking at the chart, we can notice that Apple’s RSI generally stays between 30 and 70 without breaking these boundaries significantly. This means Apple wasn’t in an extreme overbought or oversold state during this period. In early 2022, the RSI dropped close to 30, indicating a phase where the stock was somewhat oversold. This could have been a potential buying opportunity according to RSI logic.

            * JPM (JPMorgan) : 
             During the analyzed period, JPM's RSI fluctuates between approximately 50 and 85. It regularly exceeds the 70 threshold (overbought), especially in 2023 and early 2024, where it even reaches 75-80. This suggests that JPMorgan experienced strong demand phases, potentially linked to good earnings or a favorable context for the financial sector. The RSI never dropped below 30, indicating no marked oversold phase.

            * MSFT (Microsoft) : 
            For Microsoft, the RSI shows more pronounced movements compared to Apple. The RSI sometimes exceeds 70 (overbought zone) and falls below 30 (oversold zone), especially during 2022. This suggests that Microsoft experienced stronger rally phases followed by sharper corrections. This behavior could provide interesting signals for RSI-based trading.

      * MA20 and MA50 Moving Averages :
          * AAPL : On March 15, 2022, the closing price (151.95 USD) was trading below its MA20 (159.52 USD) and MA50 (164.14 USD), confirming a bearish trend that lasted for most of 2022. From January 2023, the chart shows a sustained breakout above the MA50, along with a bullish crossover of the MA20 above the MA50, signaling a clear trend reversal. A buy signal could have been triggered on the bullish crossover in early 2023, with a stop-loss placed below the MA50.

          * JPM : Over the 2023-2024 period, JPMorgan's price consistently traded above both moving averages, with the MA20 constantly above the MA50. This pattern is typical of a strong uptrend, except for a brief weak phase in late 2022 when the price temporarily dropped below both averages. This was followed by a marked acceleration, with a widening gap between the price and the MA50. With a well-established and sustained uptrend, a long-term buy-and-hold strategy is appropriate, with possible accumulation on technical pullbacks toward the MA20.

          * MSFT : In 2022, the stock experienced clear breakdowns below the MA50 with larger gaps than those observed for Apple. In 2023, the MA20 acted as dynamic support confirming higher volatility than AAPL over the analyzed period. Given this volatile profile, a more active trading approach is recommended: buy when the RSI is in oversold territory and the price tests the MA20, take partial profits in overbought zones, or exit on a breakdown below the MA50.

      * Bollinger Bands :
         * AAPL (Apple):
          The price frequently crossed the upper band (BB_Upper), indicating overbought phases often followed by corrections.Frequent returns to the middle band (BB_Middle) show that the central trend acts as a balance line. Apple exhibits then high volatility, with frequent overbought and oversold signals. 

         * JPM (JPMorgan Chase):
          The price often tested the lower band (BB_Lower), signaling oversold phases. Crossings of the upper band are less common, reflecting a more defensive dynamic. JPM shows then more oversold signals than overbought ones, indicating moderate volatility but sensitivity to corrections. 

         * MSFT (Microsoft):
         The price often stayed close to the middle band (BB_Middle), with occasional moves toward the upper band.Contacts with the lower band are rare, reflecting a dominant bullish trend. Microsoft shows then a more stable structure, with occasional overbought signals but few oversold ones.
